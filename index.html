About Optants Esports

Follow the Verified Optants Esports Twitter!

Optants Esports Is a professional Esports, apparel and entertainment organization, based out of Ottawa, Canada centered on reprofiling the Esports & the Entertainment industry by offering quality content, top-notch competitive gameplay and unparalleled streetwear. Optants Esports is growing tremendously fast across all social media platforms, while constantly pushing out content and entertainment videos to platforms like YouTube, Twitter and Instagram. Optants’ members & organization have accumulated over 100,000,000 views across all platforms.

Optants mostly has a large and talented Fortnite roster, however optants is working on rosters for many different games such as Apex Legends, Valorant, Call of Duty: Warzone, Minecraft, and Counter-Strike: Global Offensive. Our competitive players work hard to place highly in tournaments, LAN events, and community events while our content creators and streamers create entertaining and passionate content for viewers enjoyment.
